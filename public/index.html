How to make your data persist on Railway

Without this setup, data is wiped every time Railway redeploys. Follow these steps:

  1. Go to your Railway project dashboard
  2. Click your raha-pos service
  3. Click Settings tab
  4. Scroll to Volumes → click Add Volume
  5. Mount path: /data
  6. Then go to Variables tab and add:
    DATABASE_PATH=/data/raha_pos.db MASTER_PASSWORD=your-secret-password-here
  7. Click Deploy — your data will now survive forever
